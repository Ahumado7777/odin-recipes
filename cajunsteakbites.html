Cajun Steak Bites

Description

Spicy Cajun flavours, combined with the richness of garlic butter, you'll want these delicious steak bites over and over again.

Ingredients

  • Two round or striploin steaks.
  • Three tbsp of butter.
  • Two tbsp of Cajun seasoning.
  • One large ziplock bag.
  • One clove of minced garlic.
  • Two tbsp of cooking oil.

Steps

  1. Cut the steaks into 1" cubes.
  2. Put the cubed steak into the ziplock bag
  3. Add the two tbsp of Cajun seasoning into the bag.
  4. Shake well until the steak is evenly coated.
  5. Put the steak into a lightly oiled hoy frying pan.
  6. Cook the steak until all sides are seared.
  7. Add two tbsp of butter and the minced garlic.
  8. Cook to personal preferences, i.e. medium.
  9. Remove from the pan and serve with fries or whatever you like.
  10. Enjoy!
