Burgers

Description

Thick or thin, made on the grill or stovetop, this is the best and easiest recipe for perfect burgers every time!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef.
  • 1 egg yolk.
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  • 1/2 tsp salt.
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder.
  • 1/2 tsp chili pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a bowl and combine. Don't overdue the mixing.
  2. Form into patties.
  3. Lightly oil a hot frying pan and place the patties.
  4. Sear the burgers for 45 seconds for each side.
  5. Place the patties on a baking sheet and put them in the oven for 8 minutes at 400F.
  6. Remove the burgers from the oven and assemble them on your bread of choice or eat them as is.
  7. Enjoy!
