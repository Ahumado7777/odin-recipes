Lasagna

Description

The best lasagna recipe is made with ingredients that you know, and it's not difficult at all!

All you'll need for this easy lasagna recipe is one pan, one bowl and a baking dish!

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Onion
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Beef ands Sausage

Steps

  1. Cook beef and sausage until browned. Drain excess fat. Set aside
  2. Combine egg, tomatoe paste, chopped onion, minced garlic and parsley in a bowl.
  3. Mix thouroughly.
  4. Add the tomatoe mixture to the beef and sausage and mix thouroughly.
  5. Add a layer of beef mixture to the baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of Mozzarella cheese.
  7. Add a layer of Lasagna Noodles.
  8. Repeat this process untill all the beef mixture is used.
  9. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  10. Put the lasagna in the oven for 30 minutes at 400F.
  11. Cook until golden brown.
  12. Serve with Garlic Toast.
  13. Enjoy!
